Desert Ideas:
1. OASIS ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - New blocks:
    - Palm Log
    - Stripped Palm Log
    - Palm Wood
    - Stripped Palm Wood
    - Palm Planks
    - Palm Slab
    - Palm Stairs
    - Palm Fence
    - Palm Fence Gate
    - Palm Door
    - Palm Trapdoor
    - Palm Pressure Plate
    - Palm Button
    - Palm Leaves
    - Palm Sprout
    - Palm Sign
    - Palm Hanging Sign
    - Palm Boat
    - Palm Boat with Chest
    - Coconut
      : Has three stages and starts attached to the palm tree;
      : As an item, it can only be placed on the palm tree logs to grow new ones;
      : When it drops after the third stage, it stays on the ground until broken, where it drops 2-4 coconuts;
      : Can be used in compositing.
    - Reeds
      : Underwater plant, spawning in oasis;
      : Comes in two sizes, one in 1.5 height and the other in 3;
      : Can be used to craft Paper, like Sugar Cane;
      : Can be used as fuel;
      : Can be used as food for Camels.
    - Tall Reeds
      : Just like Tall Grass, it does spawn naturally, but it's impossible to get this block in Survival.
    - Coir Mat
      : 6 Coir in a Trapdoor formation;
      : Like a Sign, you can write something on it.
    - Silt Mud
      : Sandy-brownish texture of normal Mud;
      : Can be crafted by Mud + Sand / Red Sand;
      : Can also be crafted to Packed Mud;
      : Appears in the oasis area, underwater mixed with sand and on land, near the water;
      : If placed below of Farmland blocks, the moisture of these blocks rises even more, making crops grow faster.
    - Cracked Mud Bricks
    - Doll's Eyes
      : New flower to be dug from Sniffers on Silt Mud / Mud or Sand from Beaches;
      : Can be used to craft a new Beige Dye.
    - Beige Banner
    - Beige Bed
    - Beige Candle
    - Beige Carpet
    - Beige Concrete
    - Beige Concrete Powder
    - Beige Glazed Terracotta
    - Beige Shulker Box
    - Beige Stained Glass
    - Beige Stained Glass Pane
    - Beige Terracotta
    - Beige Wool

  - New items:
    - Coconut Bowl
      : Crafted with 3 coconuts in the same form as a normal Bowl;
      : You can craft every type of food you can normally craft with the Wooden Bowl, except with the Coconut Bowl,
        every item has an increase of 4 on hunger;
      : On Suspicious Stews with the Coconut Bowl, the duration of the effect is 5x of that from a normal Bowl.
    - Coconut Beetroot Soup
    - Coconut Mushroom Stew
    - Coconut Rabbit Stew
    - Coconut Suspicious Stew
    - Coconut Milk
      : Coconut Bowl + Coconut;
      : Restores 4 hunger;
      : Clears effects just like the Milk Bucket.
    - Coir
      : 3 String in Bucket form + Coconut in the middle;
      : Is a very good compostable resource, having an 100% chance for composting.
    - Reed Flute
      : 2 Reed (#) + 1 Stick (A) + 1 String (B):
        . . #
        . # B
        A . . ;
      : When played, any neutral mob on an 8 block radius that was previously attacking the player stops the attack.
    - Doll's Eye Frond
      : The seed for the Doll's Eyes
    - Beige Dye
    - Beige Bundle

  - New generation:
    - Palm trees
      : The log top will not feature normal tree rings since palm trees do not contain annual ones, instead it will
        feature a smooth surface on top;
      : Spawns coconuts.

  - Features:
    - Axolotl
      : Spawns on oases with a 60% chance.
